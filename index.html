Who we are / Our mission statement

artist4esim is an initiative to encourage eSIM donations amidst Gazaâ€™s internet blackout. Our group of artists offer a gift in the form of chibi or bust art in exchange for a proof of eSIM donation valued $15 and above.

eSIM donations go towards #connectinggaza, a crowdsourced effort helmed by @mirna_elhelbawi to facilitate eSIM donations and help Gazans access the internet. For more information about this effort, please check out gazaesims.com.

Our project is not directly affiliated with connectinggaza or the distribution of eSIMs.

Terms & Conditions

  1. Only donations made after 14th April are eligible for a gift.
  2. Requests will be handled by artists based on each artist's preferences and personal workload. Please note that you cannot pick an artist to do your request.
  3. Artists may choose to fulfill requests as busts or full-body chibis at their discretion.
  4. Details such as requesting for a specific artist or style will be omitted when requests are passed on to the artists.
  5. We reserve the right to decline requests that do not meet our criteria (specified in the request section) or the discretion of all participating artists. In such cases, we will reach out to you via email for a different request.
  6. Please allow for a 2-months fulfillment period for us to complete your gift.
  7. Completed gifts can be used for personal purposes with credit to the respective artists. Commercial use, NFTs, AI learning of our gifts are prohibited.
  8. Our gift exchange period may be stopped at any time.

FAQ

As advised by gazaesims.com,

  • If eSIM has not been activated for days/weeks, reforward the email to gazaesims@gmail.com with the expiration date.
  • If eSIM is about to expire or has expired, contact the company's customer service for renewal or credit to buy a new one.
  • If eSIM is accidentally activated, contact customer service to have it be reset.

If your intended request is not listed in our criterias, feel free to DM us to check with our artists :) You can submit a form regardless, but please take note that we may email you to ask for another request if your initial one is not feasible.

Any eSIM donation accepted by #connectinggaza is eligible for a gift from us; please check with the initiative instead whether your carrier is accepted.
